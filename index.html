About me

I am a Master's student at the University of Saarland. I also work part-time at the DFKI (Deutsches Forschungsinstitut für Künstliche Intelligenz). Currently I'm looking for a job!

During my studies I have focused on Machine Learning for NLP. In particular I am interested in sequence to sequence models used for Neural Machine Translation. My thesis was related to Grammatical Error Correction, where I focused on generating artificial training data. I am also interested in language modeling, clustering, parsing, language data management, noisy user-generated data and working with low-resource languages.
